## Service Descriptions

### Interior Design
**Full-Service Interior Design**
We provide comprehensive interior design services from concept to completion. Our process begins with understanding your lifestyle, aspirations, and the unique character of your space. We develop custom design plans, select materials and furnishings, manage contractors, and oversee installation to ensure every detail reflects your vision.

**Renovation Consulting**
For clients undertaking renovations, we provide expert guidance on architectural decisions, material selections, and space planning. We work collaboratively with architects and builders to ensure the renovation honors the existing structure while creating spaces that function beautifully for modern living.

**New Construction Design**
We partner with architects and builders from the ground up, influencing floor plans, lighting design, and material selections to create homes that are both architecturally sound and personally meaningful. Our involvement from the earliest stages ensures seamless integration of design and construction.

### Home Staging
**Vacant Home Staging**
We transform vacant properties into inviting spaces that help buyers envision themselves living there. Our staging highlights architectural features, establishes flow and purpose, and creates emotional connections that lead to faster sales at higher prices.

**Occupied Home Staging**
We work with homeowners to declutter, rearrange, and depersonalize their spaces while still feeling warm and lived-in. Our occupied staging maximizes space, highlights architectural features, and creates neutral canvases that appeal to broad buyer demographics.

**Real Estate Partnerships**
We offer partnership programs for real estate professionals seeking reliable staging services. Our packages include flexible scheduling, competitive pricing, and portfolio-quality photography that helps listings stand out in competitive markets.

### West Texas Ranch Aesthetic
**Ranch Renovation**
We specialize in renovating historic ranch properties, honoring their original character while introducing modern comfort and functionality. Our approach respects traditional materials and craftsmanship while integrating contemporary amenities for today's ranch living.

**Ranch New Construction**
We design new ranch homes that feel authentic to the West Texas landscape. Our designs draw from regional architectural traditions—stone foundations, metal roofs, deep porches—while incorporating modern floor plans and sustainable building practices.

**Landscape Integration**
We design indoor-outdoor living spaces that blur the boundaries between interior and exterior. Our approach considers views, light, and the natural landscape, creating spaces that feel connected to the land and the West Texas sky.

### Design Consultations
**Initial Consultation**
Our initial consultation is a conversation about your project, your lifestyle, and your aspirations. We visit your space, discuss your goals and budget, and provide initial insights and recommendations. This consultation helps determine if we're the right fit for your project.

**Design Direction Consultation**
For clients who want guidance but plan to execute independently, we provide design direction consultations. We develop concept boards, material recommendations, and space planning guidance that you can implement at your own pace.

**Material Selection Consultation**
For clients working with other architects or builders, we provide material selection services. We help choose finishes, fixtures, and furnishings that create cohesive, beautiful spaces while respecting architectural constraints.

### Example Project Description
**Marfa Ranch House**
**Location**: Marfa, Texas
**Type**: Ranch Renovation
**Size**: 4,500 square feet
**Year**: 2023

**Description**
This historic ranch renovation honors the original 1920s structure while introducing modern comfort and sustainability. The clients, a family from Austin, wanted a weekend retreat that respected the ranch's heritage but functioned for contemporary family life.

We preserved the original stone foundation, exposed wood beams, and metal roof while updating systems, insulation, and windows for energy efficiency. The floor plan was reconfigured to create open, flowing spaces that connect to the landscape through large windows and deep porches. Materials were sourced locally—limestone from a nearby quarry, mesquite from regional sawmills, and metalwork by local artisans.

The result is a home that feels both historic and contemporary, deeply connected to its place while thoroughly modern. The clients report that the house has transformed their family weekends, providing a sanctuary that feels both familiar and new.

## FAQ Copy

### General Questions
**Q: What areas do you serve?**
A: We primarily serve West Texas, including the Marfa, Alpine, Fort Davis, and Big Bend regions. We also take on select projects in Austin, Dallas, and Santa Fe for clients seeking our specific expertise in West Texas ranch aesthetic.

**Q: What is your typical project timeline?**
A: Full-service interior design projects typically take 6-12 months from concept to completion, depending on scope and complexity. Renovation consulting projects are usually 3-6 months. Staging projects can be completed in 2-4 weeks.

**Q: What is your pricing structure?**
A: We work on both fixed-fee and hourly arrangements depending on project type. Full-service design projects are typically fixed-fee based on scope. Consulting and staging services are typically hourly. We provide detailed proposals after our initial consultation.

### Service Questions
**Q: Do you work with clients outside of West Texas?**
A: Yes, we work with clients throughout Texas and beyond who are seeking our specific expertise in West Texas ranch aesthetic. However, we prioritize projects within our core service area and travel fees may apply for distant projects.

**Q: Can you work with my architect/builder?**
A: Absolutely. We frequently collaborate with architects and builders and value these partnerships. Early involvement in the design process allows us to influence architectural decisions and create more cohesive results.

**Q: Do you handle purchasing and installation?**
A: For full-service design projects, yes. We handle all procurement, receiving, and installation to ensure quality control and seamless execution. For consulting projects, we provide guidance but clients handle purchasing independently.

### Staging Questions
**Q: What is the difference between vacant and occupied staging?**
A: Vacant staging involves bringing in all furniture and accessories to transform empty spaces. Occupied staging works with existing furnishings, decluttering, rearranging, and supplementing to enhance the space without a full furniture investment.

**Q: Do you offer real estate partnership packages?**
A: Yes, we offer partnership programs for real estate professionals with preferred pricing, flexible scheduling, and portfolio-quality photography. Contact us for more information about partnership opportunities.

## Content Guidelines

### Writing Style
- **Tone**: Warm, confident, understated
- **Voice**: First-person plural ("we")
- **Perspective**: Collaborative, not authoritative
- **Language**: Simple but sophisticated
- **Length**: Concise, respect reader's time

### Content Principles
- **Show, Don't Tell**: Use specific examples, not generalities
- **Tell Stories**: Frame projects as narratives, not specifications
- **Be Specific**: Use real details, not vague descriptions
- **Be Honest**: Acknowledge challenges, not just successes
- **Be Human**: Show personality, not corporate polish

### SEO Guidelines
- **Keywords**: Include naturally, don't keyword stuff
- **Headings**: Use keywords in headings where appropriate
- **Meta Descriptions**: Write for humans, not search engines
- **Alt Text**: Describe images for accessibility and SEO
- **URL Structure**: Clean, descriptive URLs

### Accessibility Guidelines
- **Alt Text**: Descriptive alt text for all images
- **Headings**: Logical heading hierarchy
- **Link Text**: Descriptive link text, not "click here"
- **Content Length**: Break up long content with headings
- **Language**: Plain language, avoid jargon
