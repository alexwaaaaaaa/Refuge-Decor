## Risk Management

### Risks
- **3D Scene Complexity**: 3D scene may be too complex for timeline
  - **Mitigation**: Defer 3D scene to post-launch
- **Performance Issues**: Performance may not meet targets
  - **Mitigation**: Optimize iteratively, defer non-critical features
- **Accessibility Issues**: Accessibility may not meet WCAG AA
  - **Mitigation**: Use accessibility tools, consult accessibility expert if needed
- **Timeline Delays**: Implementation may take longer than expected
  - **Mitigation**: Prioritize critical features, defer non-critical features
